Food Tips

Tip Solution
Best way to fry potatos: Slice the potatoes, soak them for 5 minutes, oil them, and fry them on a non-stick pan.
Making the best guacamole: Find the ripest avocados possible, add spices and tomato for extra flavor, add lime for more flavor and to keep it from going brown
The best spaghetti ever made: Don't add oil to your boiling water or noodles, add the pasta to the sauce...not visa versa, cook the noodles last to have the freshest noodles in your spaghetti!
Who doesn't love a refreshing smoothie, but who wants a bad smoothie? Don't use fruit juice, use some sort of milk. Add about 1/4 avocado for a richer taste. Lastly, use fruits that go well together, not just the first thing you see!
Learn to make a fantastic waffle: Start off by preheating your waffle iron while you stir your eggs, and beat your other waffle ingredients. After this, spray your waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray, and cook your waffle until it turns golden brown.
Sandwiches are great, let's make one: Use the bread of your choice. Some must need ingredients are tomato and lettuce, but make sure nothing is soggy. Put some unions, and your choice of meat inside. Toast your bread until it reaches a perfect golden state!
Salad is very healthy, but it can also be very tasty!
